How to Play Real vs AI

Game Overview

Real vs AI is an image-identification game that challenges your ability to distinguish between authentic photographs and AI-generated images.

What are AI-generated images?

AI-generated images are either completely AI-generated or AI-assisted composite images/photo composites using AI. These images are created using AI models that analyze and manipulate elements from real photographs, combining them into a new visual design.

These can involve processes like image stitching, where AI blends parts of various photos together seamlessly. Some AI tools use techniques like style transfer, which overlay artistic elements onto a base image, or image inpainting, which fills in missing parts of photos creatively.

Such creations retain elements of realism due to the use of real photos but are often modified or augmented to suit specific creative purposes.

Game Modes

Single Player Mode

How to Play

In Single Player mode, you'll be shown two images side by side - one real and one AI-generated. Your task is to identify which image is the real one.

Difficulty Levels
  • Easy: 20 rounds with 5 lives
  • Medium: 50 rounds with 3 lives
  • Hard: 100 rounds with only 1 life
Scoring

Each correct answer adds 1 point to your score. Each incorrect answer costs you 1 life. The game ends when you run out of lives or complete all rounds.

Bonus Game

In Easy and Medium difficulties, every 10 turns you'll have the option to play a bonus mini-game if you have less than the starting number of lives. The bonus game shows you four images, one of which is real. If you correctly identify it, you gain a life. If you're wrong, you lose half your score (rounded up). You can always choose to skip the bonus game if you prefer.

Play with Friends (Multiplayer)

How to Play

In Multiplayer mode, you compete with friends to see who has the best eye for real vs AI images. The game starts once at least two players have joined.

Joining a Game
  1. One player creates a game and receives a session ID
  2. Other players use that session ID to join the same game
  3. Up to 4 players can join a single game
Gameplay

All players see the same images for 10 rounds. Each player makes their own selection independently. The player with the most correct answers at the end wins!

Notes

Players can join a game that's already in progress. If players leave and only one remains, the game will continue.

Endless Mode

How to Play

Endless Mode is for those who want a true challenge. Keep playing as long as you can maintain your lives!

Setup

You start with 3 lives and can play indefinitely until you run out of lives.

Scoring

Your score is the total number of correct answers. High scores in Endless Mode are tracked on a separate leaderboard.

Achievements

As you play, you can earn various achievements and badges that will be displayed on your profile:

Gameplay Achievements

Earn these by completing games at different difficulty levels

Score Achievements

Reach impressive score milestones across any game mode

Social Achievements

Win multiplayer games against friends

Tips for Success

  • Look closely at fine details like hair, teeth, and fingers - AI often struggles with these
  • Check for unnatural lighting or shadows that seem off
  • Examine textures and patterns for inconsistencies
  • Look for unusual distortions or warping in the background
  • Trust your instincts - if something looks "off" about an image, it might be AI-generated
